● Rest Day
Tuesday
Active Recovery
Light movement only — let your muscles rebuild
Recovery Day
Aim for 7,000+ steps of light walking today. No gym needed.

A 10–15 min walk after meals lowers blood sugar spikes by up to 30%.
Soreness is normal. If your chest or back is sore from Monday, that's expected. Severe pain or dark urine — call your doctor.

● Full Rest
Sunday
Full Rest
Recovery is where the gains actually happen
Rest & Recharge
Full rest today. Sleep, eat well, let your body rebuild. Aim for 7–8 hours of sleep — poor sleep directly raises blood sugar.

A short evening walk is welcome but not required.
